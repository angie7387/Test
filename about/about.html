Fruit Sticker Archive
About

While this collection is digital, all stickers come from the physical world. They were first preserved in this outdated planner, with no rhyme nor rhythm. Just pure chaos and passion.

ABOUT

This website acts as an archive of fruit stickers from Angie Li’s personal collection, acquired between 2021 and present day. This archive showcases an often overlooked visual ephemera that traces national histories, cultures, and of course, their local fruits.



PURPOSE

→ Elevate the often overlooked tiny fruit sticker
→ Preserving artifacts of design ephemera
→ Highlighting the everyday appearances of design
→ Sparking connection through shared interest in fruit stickers
